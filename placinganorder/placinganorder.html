Ordering Films & Books from AIF
To order a copy of any of the films listed in the Filmmakers / films section please download and fill out an Order Form (click to download)
You can e-mail your order form to theodore[at]aotearoaindependentfilm.com and I'll get back to you asap to sort payment and access to download and view your purchases.
Alternately you can give Theo a call directly, and let him know which films you're interesteed in purchasing and he'll help you sort out the details.

Book purchases will be shipped once payment has been recieved. Please note that films are currently only avilable as digital downloads (no phyisical DVDs/BluRays yet, sorry).
If you have issues downloading large files then get in touch and we'll try sort out mailing you a USB stick or something.
