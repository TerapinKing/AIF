An unsent e-mail - 21st May 2025

Hi ------,

I hope you are doing well.
I enjoy your films very much. Thank you for making them avilable on your website.

Do you believe that film has a lineage of 'outsider artists' like film of music does?

Do you believe that films will continue to be popular entertainment for the foreseeable future or are we seeing an inevitable sea-change into cinema becoming a niche art-form like jazz or poetry?

What do you like to eat for breakfast?
Do you have the same breakfast every day?

Which version of us is the real us? Who we are along in our heads or who we are when relaxed and distracted by others? I sometimes feel like those two people inside do not recognise each other.

Do you believe that an e-mail can be art? Poetry?

Is it possible to make art that is not self-conscious?

Please do not feel obligated to answer all or any of these questions. I just like writing them and thought you might enjoy reading them.

Again, I hope you are doing well.


my name is
f. theodore elliott
