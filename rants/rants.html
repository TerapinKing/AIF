On Being A Failure - 28th May 2025

'You suck' - me

My aim with this is not to inspire you
My aim is to disinspire you
You are trying to make a good film.
Consider yourself a faluire beofre you start
You're film will not be good
You will not learn anything and you will not improve
I hate that quote from Beckett
“Ever tried. Ever failed. No matter. Try again. Fail again. Fail better.”
(mostly, i suppose, cos it totally misrepresents Bectkett's meaning).
If you're film was a faulire, would you still make it
If it turns out bad (which it will), would you still make it
If noo ne cares about it (which they won't), would you still make
If people watch it and hate it (and hate you for making it) (which, if you're one of the very few lucky ones who manages to get people to see their film, they will) would you still make it

My aim with this is not to inspire you
My aim is to disinspire you
You are trying to make a bad film.
Consider yourself a faluire beofre you start
Your film will not be interesting
Your film will not be funny

Don't rush to failure, you're already there.
Don't be proud of failure, there's nothing here
Don't aim for any kind of success (financial, career groeth, recognition), you will not acheieve it

You have already acheieved everything you can

now what?
